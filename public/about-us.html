Our Story

Founded in 2024, Triniva AI was born from a simple observation: while AI technology was advancing rapidly, accessing these powerful models remained complicated and expensive for most people. We set out to change that.

Our team of AI enthusiasts and engineers worked tirelessly to create a platform that not only aggregates the best AI models but also makes them intuitive to use. Today, Triniva AI serves thousands of users across India and beyond, helping them leverage AI for education, work, and creativity.
