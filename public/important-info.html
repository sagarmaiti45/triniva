About Triniva AI

Triniva AI is a powerful multi-model AI chat platform that provides access to various state-of-the-art language models through a unified interface. Our platform leverages the OpenRouter API to offer you flexibility in choosing the best AI model for your needs.

Available AI Models

We offer access to a diverse range of AI models, each with unique capabilities:

GPT Models

  • GPT OSS 20B (Free) - Open-source GPT variant with vision support
  • GPT-4o Mini - Efficient model for quick responses
  • GPT-5 Mini - Next-gen compact model with vision
  • GPT-5 - Most advanced GPT model with vision

Claude Models

Usage Guidelines

  • Use AI responsibly and ethically
  • Verify important information independently
  • Don't share sensitive personal or confidential data
  • Be aware that AI responses may contain errors or biases
  • Different models may have varying capabilities and limitations
  • Free models may have usage limits or reduced performance

Data & Privacy

Your conversations are processed through the OpenRouter API. We prioritize your privacy:

  • Conversations are temporary and session-based
  • We don't permanently store your chat history
  • No personal data is collected without consent
  • All data transmission is encrypted

For detailed information, please review our Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions.

Limitations & Disclaimers

AI Limitations:

  • AI models can make mistakes and generate incorrect information
  • Responses should not be taken as professional advice (medical, legal, financial, etc.)
  • Models may have knowledge cutoff dates and lack current information
  • Generated content may contain biases present in training data
  • Image analysis capabilities vary by model
