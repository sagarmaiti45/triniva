Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle.

What happens if I run out of credits?

You can either wait for your monthly renewal or upgrade to a higher plan for more credits immediately.

Why do different models use different amounts of credits?

Premium models like Claude Sonnet 4 and GPT-5 are more expensive to run and provide higher quality responses, so they consume more credits (20x) compared to free models (1x). This ensures fair usage and keeps our service sustainable.

Do unused credits roll over?

No, unused credits expire at the end of each billing cycle and don't carry forward to the next month.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes, we offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for all new subscriptions. See our refund policy for details.

Can I use multiple AI models with my subscription?

Yes! Paid plans give you access to all 17+ AI models. You can switch between models anytime during your conversations.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and popular wallets through our secure PayU payment gateway.
